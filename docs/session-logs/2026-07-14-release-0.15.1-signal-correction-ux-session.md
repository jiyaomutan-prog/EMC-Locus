# 2026-07-14 - Release 0.15.1 Signal Correction UX Session

## Goal

Audit the current LAB CONSOLE experience and make equipment input/output and
signal correction understandable without the abstract measurement-engineering,
scaling, engineering-curve, or excitation vocabulary.

## Delivered

- Added a Signaux et corrections decision screen based on the representation
  supplied by the equipment: time samples or a frequency spectrum.
- Kept time conversion and frequency compensation as separate revisioned
  contracts while explaining their role, formula, limits, and provenance.
- Explained sensor excitation as alimentation et conditionnement du capteur.
- Removed internal identity entry, invented source references, and placeholder
  checksums from new measurement definitions.
- Replaced raw references, revision identities, checksums, audit operation ids,
  and server verdict wording with business labels in ordinary workflows.
- Made method creation and duplication generate their technical identities and
  use readable method/category vocabulary.
- Added French CSV headings while retaining old machine headings on import.
- Made Playwright sequential because all browser workflows intentionally share
  one reset local SQLite agent.
